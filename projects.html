PERSONAL TIMELINE

As someone who does not usually post on social media but loves documenting memories, I created a timeline to complite my life's events post-graduation. I began exploring photography as a new creative hobby over the summer and combined it with my journaling in this project to look back on everyday events that bring me joy. I hope this project offers others a look into my life and who I am.

Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

LAST BITE

Each year, 119 billion pounds of food is wasted in the United States alone. We can minimize food waste by knowing what's left in our fridge/pantry, when it will expire, and how we can create delicious meals that will leave you wanting every last bite. Last Bite is a one stop app to track when food is expiring, discover new recipes, and shop for the groceries you are low on.

Skills: Figma

FRUITFULL

How often does a loved one, a relative, a or a friend cross your mind? And how often do you actually reach out when something reminds you of them? When life gets busy or when there is a big transition like moving away for college, it can be difficult to maintain meaningful relationships. Families that primarily express their emotions through acts of service are challenged to find new ways to build upon their relationships. Fruitfull is a low stake and playful app to help families and friends feel connected through text and audio messages sent with handpicked fruit.

Skills: Figma
